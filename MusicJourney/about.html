My Musical Journey

I grew up with a passive relationship to musicâ€”I didn’t dislike it, but I wasn’t particularly engaged with it either. I mostly focused on lyrics and vocals, not the music itself.

Everything changed when some online friends introduced me to music production using FL Studio, a digital audio workstation. Initially, my projects were far from impressive; I lacked the knowledge and ear for music. Despite this, I continued creating, learning, and experimenting. Through countless tutorials, mistakes, and hours of practice, I gradually improved.

Progress

Seeing the progress I made over time was incredibly rewarding. My early projects were noticeably different from those I produced after a year of dedicated learning. The growth in my skills and the quality of my work gave me a profound sense of accomplishment.

Today, I’m a vastly improved producer compared to four years ago. My taste in music has evolved, and I’ve made significant strides in music theory, mixing, and melody creation. My style has notably changed since discovering electronic dance music and ambient soundtracks. I now focus on creating ethereal tracks, including ambient, house, and techno, as well as cinematic soundtracks that I hope to see in films.

Today

While I focus on these genres, I still explore pop and hip hop if inspiration strikes. I remain open to diverse musical ideas and continue to experiment with different styles. Although I’ve made considerable progress, I’m still developing my piano skills, which are currently at a beginner to intermediate level.
