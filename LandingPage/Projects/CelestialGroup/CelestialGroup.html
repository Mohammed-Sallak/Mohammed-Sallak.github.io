Earth, Pluto, and the Sun.

This is our Beautiful Earth
  1. Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  2. It has one natural satellite, the Moon.
  3. Approximately 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water.

Our Friend Pluto.
  1. Pluto has a day that lasts about 6.4 Earth days, this is because its rotation is much slower compared to Earth.
  2. Pluto is not a planet, rather it is classified as a dwarf planet
  3. Pluto was discovered in 1930 by astronomer Clyde Tombaugh, and its name was suggested by an 11-year-old girl named Venetia Burney.

And our Cherished Sun.
  1. The Sun makes up more than 99% of our solar system's mass.'
  2. Sunlight takes about 8 minutes to reach us, which means if the sun suddenly dissapeared, we wouldn't know for 8 minutes.'
  3. The Sun is so Massive, that it can fit 1 million Earths inside it.
